# POTIONS

*they say everything is better in the east.*

A short 2D pixel sidescroller. On the way out it's a power fantasy — grab potions, get faster, hit harder, cut through everything in your path like a buzzsaw. The way back is slower, grayer, and quieter, and you finally see what — and who — you were cutting through.

The game never explains itself. That's the point.

## Play

**[Play it now →](https://fairley46.github.io/potions/)**

Or open `index.html` in any browser. No build, no dependencies — one file.

**Controls:** Arrows / WASD move · Space jump · J attack · M mute · Enter start

Sound on recommended. One melody, two lives: forward and driving on the way out, reversed and slow on the way back. Days are counted between runs.

## What the game watches (without telling you)

Everything is optional, and everything is remembered:

- Every potion can be taken — or jumped over. Walking past counts.
- Every fight can be won — or walked away from. The people you don't hurt speak differently later.
- The way back will offer it to you again. More than once. In more than one way.
- There is more than one ending. The hardest route to play is the truest one.

## Design rules

1. The game never says what it's about. Not once.
2. Every mechanic must double as metaphor, or it's cut.
3. Add investment, not length.
4. You can't die on the way back. You survive that part — that's the point.
