# POTIONS

*A five-minute journey there, and back.*

A 2D pixel sidescroller prototype. On the way out it's a power fantasy — grab potions, get faster, hit harder, cut through everything in your path like a buzzsaw. The way back is slower, grayer, and quieter, and you finally see what — and who — you were cutting through.

The game never explains itself. That's the point.

## Play

Open `potions.html` in any browser. No build, no dependencies — one file.

**Controls:** Arrows / WASD to move · Space to jump · J to attack · M to mute · Enter to start

Sound on recommended. One melody, two lives: forward at 146 BPM on the way out, reversed at 60 BPM on the way back.

## Design rules

1. The game never says what it's about. Not once.
2. Every mechanic must double as metaphor, or it's cut.
3. Add investment, not length.
4. You can't die on the way back. You survive that part — that's the point.
